About Keto Bomb Recipes

Keto Bombs are high-fat, low-carb snack recipes.

The ingredients are clean, sugar-free, and meant to provide energy without spiking blood sugar.

Keto refers to the Ketogenic Diet, popular today as a nutrition philosophy. This scientific diet has been implemented historically as a therapy for conditions like epilepsy, type II diabetes, and cancer recovery. It is effective at keeping blood sugar levels even throughout the day.

You can find more information about the history of the ketogenic diet in this article fromt the US National Center for Biotechnology Information.

If you'd like to join a ketogenic community, look into Keto Reddit.
