Chocolate Nut Butter Cup Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 6oz dark chocolate, 80% or higher
  • 1/2 cup nut butter
  • 2 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 Tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil
  1. Mix together the nut butter, sweetener, butter, and salt.
  2. Melt together the chocolate, coconut oil, and vanilla.
  3. Prepare a mini muffin pan with paper liners.
  4. Pour half of the chocolate into 12 cups. Evenly add a dollup of nut butter ontop of each, then cover with the rest of the chocolate.
  5. Refrigerate until set, and store refridgerated.

12 servings
