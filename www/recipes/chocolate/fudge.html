Fudge Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick butter (1/2 cup)
  • 2 oz unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  1. In a pan over low heat, melt the butter and chocolate. Then gently blend in the vanilla and sweetener.
  2. Place the cream cheese in a mixing bowl, and add the chocolate mix.
  3. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Freeze or refrigerate until firm.

24 servings
