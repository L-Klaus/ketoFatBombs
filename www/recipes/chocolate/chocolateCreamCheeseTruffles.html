Cream Cheese Truffle Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 Tbsp espresso coffee
  • 1/2 tsp heavy whipping cream
  • 2 Tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  1. Beat together the cream cheese and heavy cream.
  2. Beat in the sweetener, espresso, and half of the cocoa powder.
  3. Let rest in the refrigerator until you can easily shape the mixture.
  4. Scoop out 12 equal balls, then roll in the remaining cocoa powder. You can also roll in unsweeted coconut, chopped walnuts, etc.
  5. Refrigerate until set, and store refridgerated.

12 servings
