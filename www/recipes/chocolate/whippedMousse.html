Whipped Mousse Bomb

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz butter, softened
  • 2 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa poweder
  • 1 Tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  1. Mix together butter and cream cheese.
  2. Mix in sweetener and cocoa.
  3. Whip cream in a seperate bowl. Blend cream into the butter cocoa mix.
  4. Whip entire mixture until desired texture.
  5. You can store these individually in paper cups, or place in a brownie pan and slice into servings
  6. Refrigerate until set, and store refridgerated.

4 servings
