Easy Cream Pudding Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1 box sugar-free jello pudding, any flavor
  • 3 cups heavy whipping cream
  1. Whisk the pudding powder with the whipping cream.
  2. If you want more of a mousse than a pudding, whip after mixing.

12 servings
