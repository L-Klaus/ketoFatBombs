Maple Bacon Cheesecake Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pecans
  • 2 slices of bacon, cooked
  • 6 tbsp cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 5 tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp maple extract
  1. Prepare 4 small containers (ramekins, jars, mugs).
  2. Pulse pecans and bacon in a food processor until it begins to stick together, or finely chop the pecans and bacon.
  3. Divide this mix evenly between the four ramekins, pressing it down, and reserving some for sprinkling on top.
  4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese and swerve, then the cream, vanilla extract, and maple extract.
  5. Add the cream cheese mix on top of the bacon mix, then decorate with the reserved bacon mix.

4 servings
