Butter Cookie Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup blanched almond flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 3 tbsp salted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F, and line a baking sheet.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients in a bowl until it forms a soft dough.
  3. Roll the dough into 1" balls, and place them on the cookie sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Using a fork, flatten the cookies.
  5. Bake until golden, for about 8-10 minutes.
  6. Cool for a few minutes on the sheet before transferring to a rack to cool.

12 servings
