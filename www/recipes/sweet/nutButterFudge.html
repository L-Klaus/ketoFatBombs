Nut Butter Fudge Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cream cheese
  • 3 Tbsp espresso coffee
  • 1/2 tsp heavy whipping cream
  • 2 Tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  1. Melt the coconut oil.
  2. Stir in the peppermint and sweetener.
  3. If you want to make layered bombs, pour half of the mixture into your mold of choice, and let solidify in the refrigerator.
  4. Mix the cocoa into the rest of the oil, and add to the top of the first layer.
  5. Refrigerate until solid, and store refridgerated.

6 servings
