Coconut Macaroon Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp powdered sweetener, like Swerve
  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 3 egg whites
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. Chill a bowl in the frezer for mixing the egg whites
  3. Mix the almond flour, coconut, and swerve until well blended.
  4. Mix the coconut oil and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  5. Using your chilled bowl, whisk the egg whites until very stiff (stiff peaks).
  6. Gently fold the egg whites into the flour mix, without deflating the whites.
  7. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Scoop the mix into ten round equal balls, and place them on the cookie sheet evenly spaced.
  8. Bake at 400°F for 8 minutes or until they just start to brown. Cool on the pan for 5 minutes before removing.

10 servings
