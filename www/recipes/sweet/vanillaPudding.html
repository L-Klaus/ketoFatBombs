Vanilla Pudding Bomb

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Tbs vanilla extract
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 3 Tbs powdered sweetener, like Swerve

*Note: need to use the egg whites? Try these coconut macaroons *

  1. In a saucepan over med-low heat, add the heavy cream and vanilla extract. Heat until warm but not boiling. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg and sweetener until lightened.
  3. Slowly slowly drizzle the warm cream into the egg mix as you continue to whisk.
  4. Continue to whisk until the mixture is thick enough to coat a spoon.
  5. Place in a storage container and refrigerate for 1-2 hours. You may want to place cling wrap over the surface of the pudding to keep the texture uniform.

4 servings
