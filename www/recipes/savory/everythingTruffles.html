Everything Cheese Truffle Bombs

Ingredients:

  • everything bagel seasoning, as needed (ex: Trader Joe's 'everything but the bagel sesame seasoning blend'
  • 8oz soft cheese like goat cheese or cream cheese
  1. Form the cheese into balls, 1oz. each.
  2. Roll the cheese in the seasoning.
  3. Refrigerate.

8 servings
