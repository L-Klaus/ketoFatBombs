Crab Rangoon Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 170g crab meat
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 slices of cooked bacon, chopped
  1. Mix the cream cheese, mozzarella, crab, garlic, onion powder, salt and pepper.
  2. Roll the mix into one tbsp. balls, and roll each in the bacon.
  3. Store in the fridge.

24 servings
