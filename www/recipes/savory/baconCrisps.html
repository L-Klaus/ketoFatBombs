Bacon Crisps Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 5oz shredded parmean (85%)
  • 3 strips cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp coarse kosher salt
  1. Preheat the oven to 400 °F and line two baking sheets.
  2. Mix together all ingredients.
  3. Scoop up one tablespoon at a time, place on the mat and flaten.
  4. Bake for 4-5 minutes until the cheese is melted.
  5. Cool enought to crisp the cheese before eating.

12 servings
