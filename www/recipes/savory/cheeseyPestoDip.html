Crab Rangoon Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp basil pesto
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesean cheese
  • 10 olives, sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • keto friendly chip replacements: veggie slices like cucumber or bell pepper, parmesean cheese crisps, or bacon.
  1. Mix the cream cheese, pesto, cheese, olives, salt and pepper.
  2. Store in the fridge.

12 servings

(Just for the dip)
