Butter Burger Bombs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (85%)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2oz hard cheese (like cheddar, jack, parmigiano reggiano)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 °F and place a 24 cup muffin pan on top of a rimmed cookie sheet.
  2. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  3. With equally divided portions of each ingredient, each muffin tin should be layered with: beef, butter, beef, cheese, then beef (They should look like stuffed meatballs).
  4. Bake for 10 minutes.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes.

12 servings
